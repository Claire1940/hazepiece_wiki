<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Best Pairings and Combo Flow">

Dark Blade becomes much more threatening when you stop treating it like a solo weapon and start treating it like a finisher. The source data points to stun-heavy fruits such as Ice, Dark, and Rumble, and that advice makes sense. Dark Slash creates space and forces movement; Thousand Slices then cashes out when the target has fewer escape options. If you can control the opponent’s reaction, the sword’s damage becomes much easier to land.

<Callout type="tip" title="Combo Rule">
Use Z to force movement, then confirm X once the opponent spends mobility, blocks late, or gets caught in a stun.

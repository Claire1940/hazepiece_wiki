<Section icon={Map} title="How to Get the Haze Piece Katana">

The **haze piece katana** is the very first weapon most aspiring swordsmen will acquire in the game. While it matches base combat damage, it is highly recommended to purchase it early to start leveling up your Sword stats.

**Video Highlights:**
- **Starter Location**: Finding the Sword Shop on Starter Island.
- **Buying Process**: Talking to the NPC merchant next to the Fruit Dealer.
- **Skill Showcase**: Demonstration of Lion Song and Air Slash.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="Aw9avXHW6eU" title="HOW TO GET 2 SWORD STYLE + V2 | HAZE PIECE TUTORIAL" />

To buy the Katana, navigate to the **Starter Island**. Look for the Sword Shop, which is situated to the right of the Fruit Dealer. The shop is easily recognizable by a wooden stand with a Katana laid flat and a large Yoru sword resting on its side. Simply speak to the Sword Dealer NPC to buy the weapon.

| Merchant Location | Item Name | Beli Cost | Rarity | Damage Output |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starter Island** | **Katana** | **$3,000 Beli** | Common | Equal to Base Melee |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Always purchase the Katana immediately upon spawning. Even though it is the weakest weapon in the game, you must level up your Sword mastery to unlock skills for higher-tier blades later.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Katana Moveset and Abilities">

The Katana features two signature abilities that allow you to deal damage from close to mid-range. Leveling up your Sword stat is required to unlock these moves.

| Skill Name | Keybind | Required Sword Level | Skill Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lion Song** | **Z Key** | **Level 30** | The user leaps forward, slicing everything in their path. |
| **Air Cut (Air Slash)** | **X Key** | **Level 50** | Throws a massive flying slash projectile toward the target. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Chop Fruit Immunity">
Keep in mind that sword users cannot damage players or bosses utilizing the **Chop Fruit** due to their passive sword immunity. This restriction also applies to the Clown Boss (with a small 1/3 bypass chance).
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Swordsman Progression: From Katana to 3SS">

Starting with the basic **haze piece katana** is only the beginning. As you advance through the First and Second Seas, you can upgrade your combat style to multi-sword techniques.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain the Katana">
    Purchase the basic Katana on Starter Island for $3,000 Beli to begin your mastery.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Two Sword Style (2SS)">
    Travel to **Logue City** (Level 350 Island) in the First Sea. Pay **$100,000 Beli** to Mr. White to learn the Dual Katana style.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade to Two Sword Style V2">
    Defeat the **Dual Swordsman** boss on the hill (10% drop chance for the book) and return it to Mr. White to unlock 2SS V2 [Aw9avXHW6eU].
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master Three Sword Style (3SS)">
    Travel to the **Second Sea** and head to the **Snowy Graveyard** (Level 2550 Island). Defeat the level 2650 **3SS Boss** (Zoro) for a 1% chance to drop 3SS.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Stat Allocation">
If you plan to transition into 2SS or 3SS, focus your stat points heavily into the **Sword** category to maximize your damage scaling.
</Callout>

</Section>

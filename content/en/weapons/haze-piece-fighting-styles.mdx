### Key Fighting Style Mechanics

Before choosing your martial art, it is important to understand how they function within the game's mechanics:

| Mechanic | Description | Combat Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stat Scaling** | Scales entirely off your **Combat Stat** points. | High stat investment directly increases M1 and skill damage. |
| **Intangibility Bypass** | Standard styles cannot damage Logia/Elemental users. | Must activate **Buso Haki** to land hits on elemental enemies. |
| **Range Limitations** | Generally features shorter reach compared to Swords. | Requires close-quarters positioning or closing moves [hJRkpC8ve4M]. |
| **Relearning Fee** | Switching back to a previously purchased style is **100% free**. | Allows flexible build swapping depending on the activity. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
If you ever want to return to the default Combat style, you can simply speak to any Trainer NPC you have previously purchased a style from to switch back for free.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtaining the Default Combat Style">
    When you first join the game, you automatically spawn with the **Combat** fighting style equipped. This style is basic, featuring only two moves, and serves as your entry-level melee option.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlocking Black Leg in the First Sea">
    Once you accumulate **50,000 Beli** and reach a comfortable level (ideally Level 250+), sail to the **Sea Restaurant (Baratie)**. Locate the **Blackleg Teacher** standing near the outer deck area and purchase the style.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Accessing the Second Sea">
    To unlock the final style, you must progress through the First Sea and unlock access to the **Second Sea** [hJRkpC8ve4M]. Ensure your character is sufficiently leveled to survive the transition [hJRkpC8ve4M].
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purchasing Electro in the Second Sea">
    Sail to **Udon Prison** in the Second Sea. Head to the outskirts of the island to find the **Bunny Girl** NPC. Interact with her and pay **450,000 Beli** to unlock the Electro style.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Electro Style Damage Profile (Max Combat Stat)

The Electro style is widely considered the strongest overall fighting style due to its heavy crowd control and utility [hJRkpC8ve4M].

- **M1 Basic Attack**: Deals **1,073 damage** per hit without buffs [hJRkpC8ve4M].
- **Electric Tackle (Z Move)**: Deals **1,609 damage**, charges forward, knocks back, and applies a brief stun [hJRkpC8ve4M].
- **Mink Discharge (X Move)**: Deals **2,502 damage** in an area-of-effect (AoE) circle [hJRkpC8ve4M].
- **Electric Slam (C Move)**: Deals **2,600+ damage** with a heavy ground slam [hJRkpC8ve4M].
- **Electro Mode (V Move)**: Buffs basic M1 attacks to **1,180 damage** and grants a temporary movement speed boost [hJRkpC8ve4M].

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Synergy">
Because of the heavy stuns on the Electric Tackle and Mink Discharge, you can easily lock bosses (such as the Mammoth Boss) into infinite stagger loops, preventing them from attacking back [hJRkpC8ve4M].
</Callout>

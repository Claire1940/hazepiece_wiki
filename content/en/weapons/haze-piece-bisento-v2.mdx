<Section icon={Target} title="Step 2: Farming the Tremor Key">

The most difficult part of obtaining the **haze piece bisento v2** is securing the **Tremor Key**. This item is a rare drop from **Aokiji (The Ice Admiral)**, located at the Marine HQ. Aokiji is a formidable opponent with powerful elemental Ice (Hie Hie no Mi) abilities that can freeze players in place.

  <Step num="1" title="Locate Aokiji">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat the Ice Admiral">

<Section icon={Zap} title="Step 3: Defeating the Tremor Girl">

Once you possess the Tremor Key, you can proceed to the summoning gate located at the back of the Marine HQ. Using the key here will spawn the **Tremor Girl**, a level 1000 Super Boss. This is the only way to obtain the V2 upgrade for your Bisento.

**Boss Encounter Details:**
- **Level**: 1000
- **Difficulty**: Very High (Higher than Aokiji)
- **Health**: Massive pool, requires sustained DPS
- **Requirement**: You **MUST** have Bisento V1 equipped when she dies.

| **Gems** | 100% | 20 Gems per kill |
| **Berries** | 100% | $15,000 per kill |

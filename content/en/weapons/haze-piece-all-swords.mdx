<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Key Sword Routes">

Sword progression becomes much smoother once you treat it like a route tree. The real goal is not only to obtain a weapon, but to remove the bottlenecks that keep you from upgrading the next one.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Choose your main damage lane">
    Decide whether you are building around sword damage, fruit damage, or a hybrid setup. Sword mains should invest in consistency first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm the prerequisite bosses">
    Focus on repeatable targets that drop the sword, material, or accessory you need. Short respawn timers are your best friend.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Raise mastery before crafting">
    Some swords only become worth the grind once you have enough mastery to unlock their real combo pressure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finish the upgrade route">
    Craft or evolve the weapon only after you have the materials, cash, and access requirements ready. That prevents dead-end farming.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Sword Route | Key Requirement | Main Farm Target | Why It Works |
|---|---|---|---|
| Shusui route | Boss drop progress | Ryummy on Thriller Boat | Reliable route for Three Sword Style setups |
| Enma route | Boss progression | Enma Boss | Important for higher-end sword crafting |
| Zenith route | Sea 2 boss access | Zenith Super Boss | Guaranteed weapon that opens later build options |
| Dark Blade V2 route | Upgrade materials and boss access | Foggy Castle upgrade path | Strong endgame payoff for committed sword players |
| Shark Blade route | Early boss drop | Shark Boss | Fastest way to move from starter weapon to real progression |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Best Sword Loadouts and Synergies">

The best sword is the one that fits the rest of your kit. A sword that pairs well with your Haki and fruit often outperforms a stronger weapon that fights your build.

<Callout type="tip" title="Build Synergy Matters">
If your fruit already covers mobility, choose a sword that adds burst or control. If your sword is slow, pair it with a fruit or Haki tool that helps you close distance.
</Callout>

| Hybrid build | Sword, Fruit, Defense | Three Sword Style V2 | Fruit with control or gap-closing | Split stats can slow scaling |

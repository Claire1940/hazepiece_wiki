<Section icon={Trophy} title="How to Obtain the Tremor Fruit">

Obtaining the Tremor Fruit requires either luck at the random roll system or a direct purchase using in-game currency or premium Robux. The Fruit Dealer NPC is the primary source for acquiring this fruit. Because of its Uncommon rarity, it appears relatively frequently in the dealer's stock rotation compared to Legendary alternatives.

For players who prefer not to spend premium currency, saving up Beli (Money) is the most reliable path. Farming bosses and completing quests on higher-level islands will quickly yield the necessary funds. If you choose to rely on the gacha system, prepare to spend a significant amount of Beli, as the drop rate remains under five percent per roll.

| Method | Cost | Probability | Availability |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fruit Dealer Roll | Variable Beli | 4.875% | Always Available |
| Fruit Dealer Purchase | 250,000 Money | 100% (When in stock) | Rotational Stock |
| Premium Purchase | 999 Robux | 100% | Permanent |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Ability Breakdown and Mastery Requirements">

The effectiveness of the **haze piece sand fruit** is tied directly to its two core abilities. Unlike high-tier fruits that require hundreds of mastery points to fully unlock, the Sand fruit allows players to access its full kit relatively quickly. This makes it an excellent choice for players who want to save their stat points for sword or melee builds while still having fruit utility.

| Move Name | Mastery | Key Bind | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Spada** | 30 | Z | Creates a sharp blade of sand that strikes enemies in a line. |
| **Sand Tornado** | 60 | X | Summons a localized sandstorm that deals multi-hit AoE damage. |

The **Spada** move is your primary single-target tool. It has a decent reach and can be used to pull mobs from a distance. The **Sand Tornado**, however, is the bread and butter of your farming routine. By grouping enemies together and unleashing the tornado, you can clear quest objectives significantly faster than using basic combat styles.

<Callout type="warning" title="Mastery Warning">
Do not neglect your Fruit stat if you plan on using Sand for damage. While the moves unlock early, their damage scales purely off your Fruit points.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Grinding Efficiency and Tier Comparison">

In the current 2026 meta, the **haze piece sand fruit** sits comfortably in the "Usable" tier. It is often compared to the Smoke fruit because both offer elemental immunity and decent AoE moves for low-level players. However, it falls short when compared to "Best" tier fruits like Buddha or Magma, which offer much higher damage ceilings and better range.

| Fruit Name | Rarity | Grinding Tier | Why use it? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sand** | Uncommon | Usable | Easy to get, Elemental immunity, decent AoE. |
| **Smoke** | Uncommon | Usable | Similar performance to Sand, good for flight. |
| **Flame** | Rare | Best | Massive damage, excellent burn effects. |
| **Buddha** | Legendary | Best | Huge hitbox, massive defense, top-tier farming. |
| **Light** | Rare | Best | Fastest travel in game, high speed attacks. |

While Sand is categorized as "Usable," it is objectively better than Common fruits like Spike or Bomb for long-term progression. The ability to ignore damage from NPCs that do not possess Haki is a game-changer for new players who are struggling to maintain their health during long quest chains.

<Section icon={Map} title="How to Obtain the Sand Fruit">

Obtaining the **haze piece sand fruit** requires a mix of luck and currency management. Since it is an Uncommon rarity, you have a much higher chance of seeing it in the Fruit Dealer's shop compared to Legendary or Mythical fruits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Visit the Fruit Dealer">
    Locate the Fruit Dealer NPC found on most major islands. Check his stock every few hours as it rotates frequently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Currency">
    Ensure you have at least 250,000 Beli. You can farm this quickly by completing the Buggy or Arlong island quests multiple times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Roll the Gacha">
    If the fruit isn't in stock, you can spend Beli to roll for a random fruit. With a 5.57% chance, you should see Sand within 20-30 rolls on average.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Mastery">
    Once consumed, head to the starter island and defeat low-level bandits to reach 60 Mastery and unlock all moves.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Target} title="Combat Strategy & Sea Beast Hunting">

The **haze piece dragon v2** is widely considered the "King of the Ocean" due to its performance against Sea Beasts. Its ability to fly close to the water's surface while maintaining high damage output makes it safer than most other fruits.

**Sea Beast Hunting Tactics:**
- **Stay Low**: You can fly close to the ground/water. Your model might clip slightly, but you won't take damage from the sea while transformed.
- **Tornado Setup**: Use the **C-Key Tornado** to lock the Sea Beast in position before unleashing your heavy hitters.
- **Max Range**: Use **Blast Breath** from maximum range to avoid the Sea Beast's roar and physical lunges.

| Boss Type | Effectiveness | Strategy |

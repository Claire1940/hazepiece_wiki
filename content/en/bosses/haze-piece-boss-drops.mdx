<Section icon={Map} title="Best Boss Farming Route by Progression Stage">

The cleanest route is to farm bosses in the order your damage can handle them. Start with fast respawn targets, move into drop-specific bosses, then only spend long sessions on super bosses once your clear speed is stable. That keeps your farming from turning into an expensive sightseeing tour.

<Callout type="warning" title="Route Control">
Do not chase every rare boss at once. A stable loop with consistent kills usually beats a scattered route with long travel gaps.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Open with fast-clear bosses">
    Start on bosses you can defeat quickly and safely. The goal is momentum: money, EXP, and a few reliable starter drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Lock in one island loop">
    Pick a small island chain and repeat it until your damage or movement improves. Tight loops reduce wasted travel.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Add drop-specific targets">
    Once your build is stable, move to bosses that drop the exact weapon, material, or accessory you want.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Save super bosses for planned sessions">
    Super bosses are best when you already know the summon item, spawn rule, and desired reward.
  </Step>
</Steps>

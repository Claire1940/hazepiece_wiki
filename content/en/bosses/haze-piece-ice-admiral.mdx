<Section icon={Swords} title="Boss Combat Mechanics and Strategy">

The boss utilizes the Ice Fruit skillset, making him a dangerous opponent at both close and medium ranges. His moveset includes freezing attacks that can temporarily lock your character in place, leaving you vulnerable to follow-up combos. Understanding his attack patterns is key to avoiding unnecessary damage.

| Attack Name | Damage Type | Effect | How to Counter |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ice Lance | Ranged | High damage projectile | Dash laterally when he winds up |
| Ice Age | AoE | Freezes players in radius | Keep distance or use block |
| Ice Surge | Line AoE | Knocks back and damages | Jump or Geppo over the path |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Ranged Weapons or Fruits">
    Using fruits with high mobility and range, such as Light or Magma, allows you to deal consistent damage while staying out of his freeze zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Bait the Ice Age Ability">
    Approach the boss briefly to trigger his AoE freeze, then quickly dash backward. Once the ability goes on cooldown, initiate your main attack combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Geppo for Vertical Safety">
    When the boss executes ground-based line attacks, use skywalk abilities to remain airborne and maintain your damage output.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Haki Integration">
Ensure your Armament Haki is active throughout the fight to boost your physical and sword-based damage output against his elemental defenses.
</Callout>

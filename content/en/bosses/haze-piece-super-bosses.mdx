<Section icon={Swords} title="Sea 1 Super Boss Summoning & Drops">

The first sea hosts five distinct super bosses. While some spawn on strict server timers, others require multi-step summoning processes involving lower-level boss farming.

### Dual Swordsman & Mace Boss
The Dual Swordsman is the easiest super boss to track, spawning automatically every hour in Logue City. The Mace Boss, however, requires you to infiltrate a secret room behind the Marine Base Town.

| Boss | Drop 1 | Drop 2 | Money Reward | Bounty Reward |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Dual Swordsman** | Green Bandana (50%) | Book (50%) | $5,000 | 1,500 |
| **Mace Boss** | Mace (100%) | Coded Mask (100%) | $5,000 | 1,500 |

### Tremor Girl & Fire Fist
Summoning Tremor Girl and Fire Fist requires dedication. To face Tremor Girl, you must defeat the Ice Admiral at Marine HQ to obtain the TremorBeard Key. For Fire Fist, you must secure a Match from the Warden at Impel Jail, light the campfire in Tall Woods, and locate four hidden marbles.

| Super Boss | Required Key/Item | Drop Rate 1 | Drop Rate 2 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tremor Girl** | TremorBeard Key | Bisento V2 (50%)* | White Orb (25%) |
| **Fire Fist** | Match & Marbles | Pearl Necklace (100%) | Gems |

<Callout type="warning" title="Bisento V2 Requirement">
You must own and equip Bisento V1 before Tremor Girl can drop the Bisento V2 upgrade. Attempting the boss without it wastes your summoning key.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Summoning Guides">

Follow these exact steps to summon the highly coveted Sea 1 super bosses without wasting time or resources.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Summoning Tremor Girl">
    Travel to Marine HQ and defeat the Ice Admiral until he drops the TremorBeard Key. Take the key to the locked pedestal on the island, insert it to trigger the server-wide announcement, and prepare for battle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Summoning Fire Fist">
    Sail to Impel Jail and farm the Warden until you obtain the Match. Head to Tall Woods, light the central campfire with the match, search the forest floor to collect 4 glowing marbles, and activate the 4 totems to spawn the boss.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Summoning Mace Boss">
    Go to Marine Base Town and defeat the Marine Leader (5% drop rate) to obtain the Lava Ore. Retrieve the Lava Key from the table near the Navigator NPC, open the secret back jail room, and insert the Lava Ore into the altar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Fast Farming Tip">
Server hopping is highly recommended for the Dual Swordsman and Enma, as their spawn timers are active across different servers. Set your spawn point at Logue City or Snowy Island to check quickly.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Sea 2 Super Bosses & Endgame Gear">

Once you transition to Sea 2, the difficulty of **haze piece super bosses** escalates dramatically. The rewards, however, include some of the strongest weapons and accessories in the current meta.

### Enma (Level 2500)
Enma spawns every 60 minutes inside the grand castle located on the hill of the first island of the Second Sea (facing Snowy Island). Boasting 250,000 HP, this boss is a major hurdle for solo players.

### Dough User (Level 3500)
The Dough User is an endgame super boss residing in the Mirror Dimension. Summoning him is a complex process:
- Obtain a Mirror Fragment (1% drop rate from the Spirit User Boss).
- Collect 10 Doughnuts (2.5% drop rate from any NPC on Cake, Doughnut, or Peanut Island).
- Talk to the NPC in front of the mirror on Doughnut Island to open the portal.
- Pay the NPC inside the Mirror Dimension to spawn the boss.

| **Dough User** | 450,000 | Dough Trident | 50% |

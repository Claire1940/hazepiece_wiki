<Section icon={Map} title="Phase 1: Obtaining the Match at Impel Down">

The journey to summon Ace begins at the formidable Impel Down prison. This high-security location is home to the Warden, the gatekeeper of the summoning ritual. To proceed, you must defeat the Warden repeatedly until he drops the Match.

The Warden is a challenging opponent for mid-level players. He utilizes high-damage melee strikes and area-of-effect abilities that can quickly overwhelm the unprepared. Farming this boss is the most time-consuming part of the process due to the extremely low drop rate of the Match item.

| Drop Item | Drop Chance | Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Match** | 1% | Essential for lighting the Jungle campfire |
| **Warden Hat** | 5% | Cosmetic and minor stat buff |
| **Beli** | 100% | Standard currency for upgrades |
| **Experience** | 100% | High yield for leveling |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
The 1% drop rate for the Match is not guaranteed within 100 kills. Some players report needing over 300 attempts. Consistency and fast clear times are vital.
</Callout>

To optimize your farm at Impel Down, focus on high-DPS builds that can stun the Warden. If playing in a group, ensure that everyone is aware that only the player who deals the most damage or lands the finishing blow (depending on current server mechanics) may have the best chance at the rare drop. Once the Match appears in your inventory, do not reset or leave the game, as you need to carry it to the next location.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Phase 2: The Jungle Pearl Puzzle">

With the Match in your possession, navigate to the Jungle Island. This island is characterized by dense foliage and central ruins. Your objective is to locate the campfire situated near the center of the island. 

Interacting with this campfire while holding the Match will trigger the next phase of the ritual. Upon lighting the fire, four mystical pearls will scatter across different corners of the Jungle. You must find all four to proceed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Light the Campfire">
    Equip the Match and interact with the unlit campfire in the Jungle ruins. A message will confirm the pearls have spread.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Scout the Perimeter">
    The pearls are usually tucked behind trees or near the rocky cliffs of the island. Check the northern and southern shorelines first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect All Four Pearls">
    Walk over each pearl to collect it. They do not take up inventory space in the traditional sense but are tracked by the puzzle script.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate the Totems">
    Locate the four small stone totems near the Ace spawn altar. Place one pearl into each totem to complete the circuit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Summoning Success">
Once the fourth totem is activated, the screen will shake, and Fire Fist Ace will spawn immediately at the altar. Be ready for combat!
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategy for Fire Fist Ace">

Fighting **haze piece ace** is a test of your ability to manage burn stacks and dodge fast projectile attacks. Ace utilizes a variety of fire-based moves, including "Fire Pillar" and "Higan" (Fire Gun). These attacks deal continuous damage over time, making health regeneration or defensive shields essential.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Ranged Strategy">
    - Keep maximum distance to avoid the Fire Pillar AOE.
    - Use fruits like Ice or Magma for consistent long-range damage.
    - Focus on chip damage while Ace is in his cooldown animations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Melee Strategy">
    - Only engage after Ace uses his "Fire Fist" dash.
    - Utilize Haki to bypass potential elemental intangibility.
    - Ensure you have high health or "Life Leech" accessories.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Ace has a significant health pool compared to standard island bosses. If you are soloing the boss, expect the fight to last several minutes. If you die during the encounter, you may lose the spawn unless a teammate keeps the boss in combat.

<Callout type="info" title="Reward Distribution">
Upon Ace's defeat, all players who contributed sufficient damage will receive 20 Gems. The Flame Fruit drop is individual and significantly rarer.
</Callout>

| **Fire Pillar** | Circular AOE | Jump or stay outside the red circle |
| **Higan** | Rapid Projectile | Continuous movement or blocking |

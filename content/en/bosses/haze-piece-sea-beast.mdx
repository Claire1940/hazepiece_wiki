<Section icon={Target} title="How to Farm Sea Beast Efficiently">

Efficient farming is mostly about removing decision fatigue. Once you know your route, the run should become repeatable: travel, locate, engage, loot, reset. That rhythm matters more than trying to force a perfect fight every time.

The goal is not to look impressive. The goal is to keep the loop clean so every clear adds value. If your route is messy, even good drops can feel slow because the travel time and recovery time eat the profit.

<Callout type="info" title="Route Rule">
If your current server, build, or party setup cannot support a clean reset, stop and rebuild the route instead of forcing another bad attempt.
</Callout>

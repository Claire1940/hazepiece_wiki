<Callout type="warning" title="Early Game Mistake to Avoid">
Do not camp one boss too long just because the drop looks rare. In Sea 1, progress speed matters more than chasing a single item with a low chance.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Super Boss Unlocks and Farming Loop">

Super bosses are where planning matters. Some spawn naturally, some require quest items, and some are tied to specific upgrade routes. The smart move is to treat them like checkpoints, not random detours. Build your route around the unlock condition first, then farm the boss only when the reward is worth the time.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Finish your Sea 1 progression loop">
    Clear quick bosses first, grab a stable weapon or fruit route, and unlock the items that feed into later summons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect summon items on purpose">
    Farm drops like Tremor Key, Match, Dragon Orb, or Mirror Fragment only when they connect to a boss you actually want.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Move into Sea 2 route bosses">
    Focus on Enma, Zenith, and Dough paths before spending too long on lower-value targets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reserve super boss sessions">
    Use longer sessions for bosses with 60-minute timers, upgrade chains, or guaranteed endgame rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Upgrade around one main damage lane">
    Stick to Fruit, Sword, or Fighting Style as your primary lane so boss kills stay fast and consistent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

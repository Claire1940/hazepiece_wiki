<Section icon={Map} title="How to Spawn the Super Boss Dough User">

To obtain the exclusive Dough-themed gear, players must summon and defeat the **Super Boss Dough User** located on **Doughnut Island** in the Second Sea. This requires a multi-step questline involving rare drops.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain the Mirror Fragment">
    Travel to **Cake Island** and battle the **Soul Boss (Big Mom)**. You must grind this boss until you receive the **Mirror Fragment**, which has a **1% drop rate**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect 10 Donuts">
    Defeat any NPCs on the new update islands (including Peanut Island and Doughnut Island). Each NPC has a **1% chance** to drop a Donut. You must gather 10 in total.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock the Mirror World">
    Bring the Mirror Fragment and 10 Donuts to the quest NPC on Doughnut Island. Handing these over grants you permanent access to the **Mirror World portal**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Summon the Super Boss">
    Enter the Mirror World and pay **50 Gems** to spawn the Super Boss Dough User. Note that this summon has a **2-hour server cooldown**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="PvP Warning">
The Mirror Fragment is a physical item in your inventory. If you are defeated by bounty hunters while carrying it, you will lose the item. Farm this in private servers or with trusted allies.
</Callout>

</Section>

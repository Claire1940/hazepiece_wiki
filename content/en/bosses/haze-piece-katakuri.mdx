<Section icon={Target} title="Mirror World Access Requirements">

To obtain the signature weapons for **haze piece katakuri**, you must enter the Mirror World. This is not a simple task and involves two distinct farming phases. First, you must challenge Big Mom on Cake Island to obtain the Mirror Fragment. This item has a notoriously low drop rate, so be prepared for a long session.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Mirror Fragment Hunt">
    Travel to Cake Island and defeat the Big Mom boss. The Mirror Fragment is a 1% drop. Once obtained, do not die, as you need to carry it to the next NPC.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find Sin on Dough Island">
    Take the Mirror Fragment to the NPC named Sin, located on Dough Island. He will recognize the fragment but require a "tribute" to grant entry to the Mirror World.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect 10 Donuts">
    Farm any NPCs on the new islands (Cake Island or Dough Island). You must collect 10 Donuts, which are also a 1% drop from standard mobs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock the Portal">
    Return to Sin with the 10 Donuts. He will unlock the Mirror World portal for you, granting access to the Dough Boss arena.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| **Observation Haki** | Haki Trainer | Required for Boss |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Defeating the Dough Boss">

Once inside the Mirror World, you won't find the boss immediately. You must locate the "Dough Fisher" (Spaceman) and pay a fee of 50 Gems to summon the "Strong Warrior"—Katakuri himself. This boss fight is one of the most challenging in **haze piece**, requiring precise timing and active Haki management.

The Dough Boss uses a variation of the Dough Fruit moveset, including a devastating version of the Dough Trident's skills. Keeping your Observation Haki active is critical to avoid being caught in his "Ultimate Buzzcut" combo, which can easily one-shot lower-health players.

| Boss Attack | Counter Strategy | Danger Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Trident Slam** | Dash laterally | Medium |
| **Rapid Mochi** | Activate Observation Haki | High |
| **Drill Thrust** | Maintain distance | Medium |
| **Buzzcut Finisher** | Use invincibility frames | Extreme |

After defeating the boss, he has a chance to drop the Dough Trident and the Katakuri Scarf. Note that these are not guaranteed drops, and there is a 2-hour cooldown before you can spawn the boss again in the same server.

<Callout type="info" title="Boss Cooldown">
The 2-hour spawn timer is server-specific. If you have the gems and donuts ready, you can server-hop to find a fresh instance and fight the boss again immediately.
</Callout>

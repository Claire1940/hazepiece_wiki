<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategy & Boss Mechanics">

Defeating Vergo requires more than just high stats; you must understand his specific attack patterns and mechanical quirks. Unlike standard mobs, bosses in this game possess unique properties that bypass your passive defenses.

The most important mechanic to keep in mind is that **all elemental (Logia) and Rubber passives do not work against bosses**. If you are accustomed to standing still and taking zero damage from standard enemies while using a Logia fruit, you must change your playstyle for this fight. Vergo's attacks will deal full damage to you regardless of your active fruit type.

Vergo heavily utilizes Armament Haki (Busoshoku Haki) to harden his body and weapon, granting him increased defense against basic attacks (M1) and boosting his physical strike damage. He moves quickly and can close distances rapidly if you attempt to run away in a straight line.

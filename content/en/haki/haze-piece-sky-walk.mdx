<Section icon={Target} title="How to build a Sky Walk route">

A clean movement route starts with one simple idea: unlock reliable mobility early, then stack race and Haki upgrades around it. That keeps your progression stable instead of turning every island hop into a detour.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Get baseline mobility first">
    Push early levels until you can access Flash Step on Clown Island at Level 40. That gives you a dependable escape and travel option before you chase better movement perks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose a race that fits your route">
    If you want pure travel comfort, Skyborne is the cleanest pick. If you want a more balanced setup, Dragonborne gives strong mobility with better long-term flexibility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Match mobility with your combat lane">
    Keep one main damage lane in mind. Fruit, sword, and fighting style builds all benefit from better movement, but none of them should be built around movement alone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Plan your Sea 2 upgrade path">
    Race V2 becomes much more valuable later. The Race Engineer route on the first Sea 2 island needs Level 2200+, a Sea Beast clear, 50 chests, Zenith Boss progress, $1M, and 100 Gems.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Stage | What to Do | Payoff |
|---|---|---|
| Early game | Unlock Flash Step | Safer questing and better island movement |
| Mid game | Pick Skyborne or Dragonborne | Better Sky Walk comfort |
| Late Sea 1 | Add Haki tools | Better fighting value while traveling |
| Sea 2 | Push race V2 | Stronger movement and combat utility |

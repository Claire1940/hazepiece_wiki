<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock and Level It">

The unlock path is straightforward, but it does have a level gate and a cash cost. That means the best route is to grind normally until you qualify, then buy Buso Haki at the right time instead of trying to force it too early. Once unlocked, its level growth comes from combat use, so you want to activate it during the fights you were already planning to take.

<Callout type="warning" title="Do Not Rush the Unlock">
If you buy random upgrades before Level 350, you slow down the route that actually matters. Save your cash for the unlock, then level Buso through real combat.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach the unlock gate">
    Push your character to **Level 350** first. That is the earliest practical checkpoint for Buso Haki.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prepare the cash cost">
    Save **$100,000** before visiting the trainer. Do not spend it on low-impact detours if you still need the unlock.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Find the trainer">
    Go to **Old Man Silver in Logue City** and buy the Buso Haki unlock.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Use it in combat">
    Keep Buso active during quest farming, boss fights, and enemy packs so its EXP progress builds naturally.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Finish the level-up quest">
    At **Flower Capital**, complete the Level 5 requirement by taking **1,000 hits** and paying **200 Gems**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Stage | Requirement | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Unlock | Level 350 + $100,000 | You gain Buso Haki access |
| Early use | Combat with Buso active | EXP builds through normal play |
| Max level quest | 1,000 hits + 200 Gems | Level 5 progress route |
| Final effect | Level 5 | **+15% damage** and **+10% damage resistance** |

The key detail is that Buso does not need special minigames or weird side conditions at the start. It just wants uptime. If you are already questing efficiently, the Haki levels come with the route instead of forcing a separate grind.

<Callout type="tip" title="Fast Leveling Habit">
Turn Buso on before every quest loop and boss pull. Passive uptime is the easiest way to avoid wasting progress.
</Callout>

<Callout type="success" title="Best Pairing Logic">

<Section icon={Swords} title="Fishing Rod Comparison: Is the Legendary Rod Worth It?">

Players can choose between three primary fishing rods to catch fish. While the game shop advertises premium rods with luck multipliers, practical testing reveals a different reality. The table below outlines the core differences between the available equipment:

| Rod Tier | Acquisition Method | Advertised Bonus | Practical Performance | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wooden Rod** | Standard Starter | None | Baseline performance | Highly Recommended |
| **Rare Rod** | In-game Progression | Moderate Luck Boost | Negligible difference | Optional |
| **Legendary Rod** | 599 Robux | +50% Luck Boost | Marginal actual increase | Not Recommended |

To understand why the Legendary Rod is widely considered a waste of Robux, we must look at how the 50% luck boost is mathematically applied. 

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="The Math Illusion">
    A **50% boost** does not add flat probability. It multiplies the base rate by 1.5. For a 1% Legendary fish, your new rate is only **1.5%**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mythical Reality">
    For a **0.1% Mythical fish**, a 50% boost only increases your final catch rate to **0.15%**, which feels virtually identical during gameplay.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Test Results">
    Controlled tests of 5 consecutive casts with both the Wooden and Legendary rods frequently yield the exact same distribution of Common and Rare fish.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="tip" title="Saves Your Robux">
Do not spend 599 Robux on the Legendary Fishing Rod. The progression value is mathematically insignificant. Stick to the basic Wooden Rod or standard in-game upgrades.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Fishing & Selling Process">

To begin your fishing journey and exchange your catches for valuable rewards, follow this straightforward process:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Your Rod">
    Purchase your preferred rod from the starter island or equip your existing rod from your inventory hotbar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find Water & Cast">
    Approach any deep ocean border on any island. Select your rod, aim at the water, and click to cast your line.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reel in the Catch">
    Wait for the bite indicator to appear on your screen, then click promptly to successfully reel in your fish.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Visit the Fish Merchant">
    Locate the Fish Merchant NPC on the docks to sell your inventory. Selling triggers your EXP, Beli, and bonus item drops.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Inventory Management">
Ensure you have open inventory slots before selling Legendary or Mythical fish, as the bonus Uncommon or Rare Devil Fruits will be placed directly into your bag.
</Callout>

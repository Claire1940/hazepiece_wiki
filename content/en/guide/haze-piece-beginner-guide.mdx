<Section icon={Swords} title="Races, Haki, and Boss Targets">

Race choice matters because it changes survivability, movement, and access to Conqueror’s Haki. **D. Clan** is the standout route because it directly unlocks **Conqueror’s Haki**, while other races lean into speed, travel, or sustain.

<Callout type="tip" title="Race Priority">
If you want a simple all-around setup, aim for **D. Clan** first. If you want movement or comfort, Dragonborne, Beastborne, or Fishborne can work well.
</Callout>

| Race / Haki | Main Effect | Best Use |
|---|---|---|
| D. Clan | +5% Health, +5% Stamina, Conqueror’s Haki; V2 improves those values | Best overall combat route |
| Dragonborne | HP, regen, stronger Skywalk, V2 glide | Tankier travel and comfort |
| Beastborne | Walkspeed and Stamina boosts | Fast movement and dash-heavy play |
| Fishborne | Swim speed, water safety for fruit users | Sea travel and safer routing |

| Boss | Key Drop | Why Farm It |

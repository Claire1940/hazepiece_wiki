You do not need a 0.1% roll to excel. A-Tier and B-Tier races offer incredible utility that often outperforms Legendary options under specific conditions, such as sea exploration or money grinding.

### Fishman: The Ultimate Utility
If you are a Devil Fruit user, falling into the ocean is usually a death sentence. The **Fishman** race completely removes this weakness, allowing you to swim freely while boosting your movement speed in water.

### Demon & Mink: The Speed Demons
For PvP players who rely on spacing and rapid movement, the **Demon** race offers a massive +50% walk speed boost. If you cannot roll a Demon, **Mink** serves as an excellent Uncommon alternative with a balanced speed and jump package.

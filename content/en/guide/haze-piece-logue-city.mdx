<Section icon={Trophy} title="The Dual Swordsman Super Boss">

The main attraction for veteran players in **haze piece logue city** is the Dual Swordsman. This Super Boss is not part of the standard quest rotation and operates on a specific spawn timer. Defeating him is a rite of passage for players looking to master dual-wielding techniques or those hunting for rare progression items.

The Dual Swordsman is known for high mobility and high-damage AoE (Area of Effect) attacks. Engaging him solo at Level 350 is possible but challenging; it is often recommended to team up with other players or use a Fruit with high stun potential.

| Boss Stat | Information |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Spawn Timer** | Approximately every 60 minutes |
| **Difficulty** | High (Super Boss) |
| **Key Drop** | Book (Used for skills) |
| **Combat Style** | Dual-wielding Melee |

The "Book" drop is highly coveted. This item is often used to unlock specific sword skills or secondary abilities that cannot be obtained through standard leveling. Because of the one-hour spawn window, Logue City often becomes a camping ground for players waiting to snipe the boss.

<Callout type="warning" title="PvP Alert">
Because the Dual Swordsman drops valuable items, the spawn area is a high-risk PvP zone. Always check your surroundings before the boss spawns to avoid being ambushed by bounty hunters.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Guide">

To maximize your efficiency in Logue City, follow this structured progression path. This ensures you don't waste time wandering or missing out on essential upgrades like Buso Haki.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Set Spawn Point">
    Locate the Set Spawn NPC near the main plaza. This prevents a long sail back from the Pyramid if you are defeated by a boss or another player.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Train Buso Haki">
    Immediately find Old Man Silver. If you have the 50,000 Beli, purchase Buso Level 1. This will significantly speed up your grinding against the local NPCs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Quests">
    Talk to the local Quest Giver to start the Level 350 mob farming. These quests provide the highest XP yield in the First Sea at this level range.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Monitor Boss Spawns">
    While completing quests, keep an eye on the central courtyard for the Dual Swordsman. If he spawns, prioritize him for the potential Book drop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Following these steps will bridge the gap between the mid-game and the transition to the Second Sea. The level 350 to 400 grind is often considered one of the slower segments of the game, so having Buso Haki active is vital for maintaining speed.

<Callout type="info" title="Fruit Dealer Strategy">
Check Cynthia's inventory every time it resets. Logue City is one of the few places in the First Sea where you can reliably find a dealer without traveling to the Starter Island.
</Callout>

</Section>

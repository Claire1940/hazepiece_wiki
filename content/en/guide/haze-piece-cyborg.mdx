### Cyborg V1 Skill Mastery Requirements
Every skill in the Cyborg Moveset requires you to level up your style's **Mastery** through combat. 

| Skill Name | Mastery Required | Skill Type & Combat Utility |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Cyborg Smash** | 0 Mastery | Close-range ground slam with heavy AoE knockback |
| **Cyborg Bullet** | 25 Mastery | Long-range projectile attack for pulling and sniping |
| **Cyborg Tackle** | 50 Mastery | Rapid forward dash that breaks enemy guards |

<Callout type="warning" title="Mastery EXP Penalty">
Keep in mind that if you farm NPCs or bosses that are more than **200 levels below** your character, you will suffer a **20% Mastery EXP penalty**. Always farm enemies close to your level!
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Upgrade to Steel Titan V2">

To truly unlock the potential of the Cyborg fighting style, you must transcend to its V2 form: the **Steel Titan**. This process requires you to venture into the late-game regions and complete specific progression milestones.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Sea 3">
    Travel to the Third Sea, where the highest-tier trainers and progression systems reside.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Grind V1 Mastery">
    Equip your Cyborg V1 Fighting Style and level its Mastery up to **at least 300**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Sage of Combat">
    Find the **Sage of Combat** NPC in Sea 3, who handles all V2 fighting style upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Gather Upgrade Materials">
    Provide the required upgrade materials and currency requested by the Sage to unlock the **Steel Titan V2** style.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Bonus Stat Points">
Reaching the maximum Mastery cap of 500 on your Fighting Style grants you up to **250 bonus stat points** (1 point for every 2 Mastery levels). Upgrading to V2 is essential for maximizing your character's overall stat pool.
</Callout>
